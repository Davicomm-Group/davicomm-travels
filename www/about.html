Who We Are

Davicomm Travels is a bespoke online travel agency that provides both business and leisure seeking travellers access to search, compare and book the very best in flight deals from the most preferred airlines.

We are a Lagos based agency operating from the heart of Lekki-Lagos, and through our partner operating in eight different countries with offices in other core markets including South-Africa, Dubai and Egypt. Davicomm Travels is a member of Davicomm Group and committed to making every travel with us an experience of a lifetime without having to breaking the bank.

Our perspective is first from affordable travels and simplifying travel booking experience for all our customers. You can find some of the very lowest fares to diverse destinations, make quick safe and secured payments online as you desire.
